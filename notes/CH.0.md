# 1.what is programing ?
Programming is the process of writing step-by-step instructions that tell a computer exactly what to do. Computers are not smart on their own. They need a human programmer to give them a clear recipe to follow to solve a problem or complete a task.

# 2.What is programing language ?

programing language is formal, structured set of instructions and grammatical rules used or written by humans  to comunicate with computers and controll their behaviour.
